About RX

SMR Engineering Firm
Spun Off from KAERI

The fastest-growing team in the nuclear industry. An engineering partner for SMR developers.

Fabless Company in
Nuclear Industry

RX Inc. is a next-generation nuclear engineering firm founded in 2023 by key engineers from the Korea Atomic Energy Research Institute (KAERI).

With team members from Conventional Nuclear EPC, we possess comprehensive expertise spanning the full scope of SMR design, analysis, testing, and licensing — built on design and licensing experience from the large-scale NPP through SMART and i-SMR.

Like NVIDIA in the semiconductor industry — a nuclear fabless company creating value through design and IP licensing is RX's business model.

Mission & Vision

Our Direction

Mission

Global Licensor in SMR Design & Licensing

Vision

Structurally Reducing SMR Cost & Timeline
